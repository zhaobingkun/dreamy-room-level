Quick plan

Focus on a clean anchor, then layer decor and leave walking space.

  • Place the front platform, stairs, and purple bench at the entrance (ground floor).
  • Install the glass façade, side columns, and “MEO STARK” sign on the middle and right side.
  • Furnish the mid-level balcony: garden beds, green crate, cat, tables, and plants.
  • Build and decorate the interior visible through the glass: sofas, coffee table, and side decor.
  • Assemble the upper deck: pool, float ring, lounge chair, umbrella, and round hot tub.
