Quick plan

Focus on a clean anchor, then layer decor and leave walking space.

  • Place the tall arched stained-glass window on the back-left wall (center-left).
  • Place the raised wooden platform and the bubbling cauldron on the left front of the platform.
  • Place the blue curio shelf to the left of the window and hang the broom and emblem on the left wall.
  • Place the central low table and the green chest behind it on the back-center.
  • Place the enchanted mirror-with-curtains on the right back wall and the small RIP stone near the right-center.
